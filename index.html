About Me

Hey, I am MouseVision!

I am a Slovak digital design student with a love for creativity in many forms.
The name MouseVison comes from a nickname my friends gave me -"Mouse"- and my desire to share my vision with the world through art, design and storytelling.

Drawing

Art was my first dive into the sea of creativity. I grew up sketching and attended art classes alongside my regular school.
While I don't draw as often as before, I still create digitally on my tablet when inspiration strikes.

Photography

Recently I discovered a passion for photography. My camera is a way to capture the quiet beauty of nature, animals, and small details that might otherwise go unnoticed.

Graphics & Design

My interest in writing later led me to design book covers for amateur writers, exploring Photoshop CS6 and building a foundation in design that I continue to expand today.

Websites

One of my first projects is building a working library website for my former school. It’s live, functional, and still in use—proof of what I can do when I combine design with purpose.

Certificates

Coursera certificates in design and related fields.
These courses gave me a strong foundation in web technologies, which I later applied when building the library website project.

  • Introduction to HTML5
  • Introduction to CSS3
  • Advanced Styling with Responsive Design
  • Interactivity with JavaScript
