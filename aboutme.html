Who am I?

I am a Slovak student. Since middle school I have been attending art classes. In general I like to draw, but I did not move any further than that. Few years later I fell in love with graphics and did online book covers for writers. Later, during one school project, when I had to work with a camera, was when I discovered I actually really enjoy photographing and video recording. Nowadays, I am also interested in programming and web design and hope to become a professional UX designer/programmer/software or game developer.

What is MouseVision?

This nickname was created with a big help of my friends. I needed something to present myself and show my skills without using my real name. Something, that has a personal meaning. People, who are very close to me like to call me a mouse or a squirell. I really adore that nickname, since I am a big fan of rodents. So I used that and connected it to the word "Vision" which in this case represents my vision of art/web design or the world in general.

Thank you for being here and letting me show you my vision of the world.
Enjoy the view!
